# CleanPep Partner Outreach

## Core Positioning

CleanPep is not asking vendors to buy rankings. CleanPep is asking vendors to prove more.

Priority evidence:

- Product identity
- HPLC purity
- Content/fill or assay
- Residual solvents
- TFA/counterion or salt-form disclosure
- Endotoxin
- Sterility or bioburden when applicable
- Batch-specific COA links or files

Affiliate links and codes are allowed only as disclosed monetization. They do not change scoring.

## Lab Quote Email

Subject: Quote request for peptide full-panel testing

```text
Hi [Lab] team,

My name is [Name] and I run CleanPep, a peptide COA transparency and vendor comparison project.

I am looking for pricing and logistics for independent peptide testing, initially for blind vendor audits and eventually for a full-panel product release standard.

Can you quote the following panel for lyophilized peptide vials?

- Identity by MS or LC-MS
- HPLC purity
- Assay/content/fill
- Residual solvents by GC-MS, including common peptide synthesis solvents where applicable
- TFA/counterion or salt-form analysis if available
- Endotoxin by LAL
- Sterility or bioburden where available
- Water content
- Heavy metals by ICP-MS if available

Please include sample quantity requirements, turnaround time, rush options, public certificate verification options, and volume pricing for recurring submissions.

Thank you,
[Name]
CleanPep
```

## Qualification Rule

Do not promote a vendor hard until one of these is true:

- They provide a real affiliate code/link plus batch-specific COAs.
- They provide enough expanded-panel data to deserve CleanPep visibility.
- CleanPep independently tests one or more representative products.

Easy affiliate approval is not enough.
