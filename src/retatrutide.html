Why This Matters

Residual Solvents

Synthesis leaves behind chemicals like acetonitrile, DMF, TFA. A "99% pure" peptide can be 40% solvent by weight. Finnrick includes solvent screens in standard panels. A 2023 Belgian study found 5/12 "high purity" vendors had dangerous levels.

Quantitative TFA

Peptides are often sold as TFA salts. Without quantitative TFA, your "10mg" vial could contain 7mg peptide + 3mg acid. Look for TFA ≤1%.

Heavy Metals (ICP-MS)

Palladium and other synthesis catalysts have strict pharma limits. Almost no peptide vendors test for them voluntarily.

Endotoxin

Bacterial contamination triggers immune reactions. Finnrick now offers this as a paid add-on. If a vendor voluntarily shows a negative, that's a huge trust signal.
