Why This Matters

Residual Solvents
The synthesis process leaves behind acetonitrile, DMF, TFA. A "99% pure" peptide can be 40% solvent by weight. A 2023 Belgian study found 5 of 12 vendors exceeded safety thresholds.
TFA Quantification
A "10mg vial" sold as TFA salt may contain 7mg peptide + 3mg acid. Without quantitative TFA, you don't know what you're actually getting.
Heavy Metals (ICP-MS)
Palladium, platinum, and other synthesis catalysts have strict pharmaceutical limits. Almost no vendor tests for metals voluntarily.
Endotoxins
Bacterial contamination causes immune reactions. Finnrick now offers endotoxin testing — very few vendors voluntarily include it. If they do, it's a massive trust signal.
Multi-Lab Verification
We cross-reference Finnrick scores with Janoshik individual COAs and community reports. One lab is good. Two+ labs = you can actually trust the data.
COA Top Color Match
Every batch has a visual identifier — the vial top color. We track the color from published COA photos so you can verify your vial matches what was tested.
