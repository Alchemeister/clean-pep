Why This Site Exists

I'm a disabled researcher who got sick of the peptide vendor "review" ecosystem. Every list is the same: "These 10 vendors are great! (affiliate link, affiliate link)." Nobody talks about what actually matters.

99% purity means nothing if your vial is 40% residual solvent. A 2023 Belgian study tested "high purity" peptides from 12 vendors and found 5 had dangerous solvent levels. Finnrick's own data reveals vendors hiding TFA levels, skipping metals testing, and quietly avoiding endotoxin screens.

This site ranks vendors on what they prove — not what they claim. All data sourced from Finnrick Analytics' public database (7,466 tests, 212 vendors, updated 22 May 2026).
