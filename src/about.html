Methodology

Affiliate pages are easy. Receipts are harder.

CleanPep exists to make vendor comparison feel less like a commission contest and more like an audit trail. The site now treats purity as only one input and rewards vendors who publish deeper, batch-specific panels.

Primary source
Finnrick public rankings and product/vendor pages. Their homepage currently states 7,829 samples from 224 vendors across 15 peptides.
Panel-depth checks
Identity match, content/fill, TFA, residual solvents, and endotoxin are scored separately from headline purity.
Source weighting
Lab-verifiable batch COAs rank above vendor screenshots, Finnrick rank rows, and community reports. Missing public proof is marked as a risk signal.
Scoring posture
CleanPep displays evidence depth; it does not certify safety, quality, legality, or medical suitability.
Disclosure: CleanPep may use affiliate links. Affiliate status does not change evidence scoring, and missing TFA, solvent, endotoxin, content, or identity proof is called out even on highly ranked vendors.
