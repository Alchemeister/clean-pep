About CleanPep
And Why This Site Exists

I'm a disabled researcher who got tired of the peptide vendor "review" ecosystem. Here's the truth: most comparison sites are just affiliate link farms. They rank vendors by who pays the highest commission, not by who actually tests their products properly.

📊 Where the Data Comes From

Finnrick Analytics is an independent lab based in Austin, TX that has tested 7,829 peptide samples across 224 vendors and 18 products — and they publish results publicly. They include the panels that actually matter: residual solvents, quantitative TFA, heavy metals by ICP-MS, and now endotoxin screening. Finnrick publishes a machine-readable summary at finnrick.com/llms-full.txt which we use as our primary data feed.

Janoshik is the oldest and most well-known independent peptide testing lab. While they don't offer bulk public data, their individual COA verification at janoshik.com/verification allows anyone to look up a specific test report. Vendors with publicly verifiable Janoshik COAs receive a "Janoshik ✓" badge on CleanPep.

Community sources — Reddit r/saferpeptides, Discord servers, and user-submitted COAs via our Google Form — provide cross-referencing, top-color verification, and vendor reputation signals that no single lab can capture alone.

No scrapers. No automated tools. No paid data. All data is publicly available and verifiable. Any row marked "⚠ Verify live" means you should check the source yourself in a browser — we'd rather be honest about what needs verification than make claims we can't back up.

📐 Scoring Methodology

Finnrick Rating
Primary signal. A/B/C confidence tiers with per-product scores.
Panel Coverage
+1 point per contamination panel shown: solvents, TFA, metals, endotoxin.
Multi-Lab Bonus
+1 if vendor has COAs from 2+ independent labs in 2026.
Freshness Bonus
+1 for a test within the last 30 days. -1 for no test in 6+ months.
Transparency
Public 3rd party COA with batch numbers and dates scores higher than self-reported data.
Top Color Match
We track the vial top color from published COA photos so you can verify your batch matches.

The Cleanliness Score is an editorial rating based on publicly available third-party test data. It is not a scientific measurement, safety certification, or endorsement. It answers one question: "How much testing transparency does this vendor actually show?"

💰 How This Site Makes Money

CleanPep is affiliate-funded. When you click a vendor link and make a qualifying purchase, we earn a commission (typically 15–30%) at no additional cost to you. We do not sell peptides, handle any products, or accept payment from vendors for better rankings. Scoring is based on public test data, not affiliate relationships. Vendors cannot buy higher scores.
